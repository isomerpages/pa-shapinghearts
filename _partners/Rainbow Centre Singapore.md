<h2>About Rainbow Centre Singapore</h2>
<p></p>

<p>Rainbow Centre, Singapore envisions a world where Persons with Disability
(PWD) live Good Lives. Through practical education, meaningful support
and effective training and partnerships, they create opportunities for
clients to fulfil their potential and participate in society. Registered
as a charity in 1992, Rainbow Centre, Singapore is an Institution of a
Public Character, and a registered society, with the Constitution of Rainbow
Centre as its governing instrument.</p>
