<h3>Rainbow Centre Singapore</h3>
<p></p>

<p>Rainbow Centre, Singapore envisions a world where Persons with Disability
(PWD) live Good Lives. Through practical education, meaningful support
and effective training and partnerships, they create opportunities for
clients to fulfil their potential and participate in society.</p>
<p>Registered as a charity in 1992, Rainbow Centre, Singapore is an Institution
of a Public Character, and a registered society, with the Constitution
of Rainbow Centre as its governing instrument. Singapore Association for
Mental Health SAMH strives to enhance the lives of persons with mental
health issues through comprehensive rehabilitation and reintegration programmes.</p>
<p>Their services support both youths and adults, accompanying them through
every stage of their recovery journey. Beyond individual care, SAMH provides
essential emotional and practical support to families and caregivers, ensuring
a holistic approach to mental wellness.</p>
