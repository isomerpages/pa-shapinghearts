<h2><strong>Shop and support a good cause</strong></h2>
<p>The physical Heartists Fair features unique art pieces and merchandise
specially made and designed by special needs artists, who we call ‘Heartists’!
Proceeds from the sale goes towards a good cause of supporting participating
Social Service Agencies and Heartists.</p>
<p><strong>More details will be updated soon.</strong>
