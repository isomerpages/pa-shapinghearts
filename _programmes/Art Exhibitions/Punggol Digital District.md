<p>Get ready to embark on an engaging and meaningful journey at Shaping Hearts
@ Punggol Digital District – ARTfully Able, with 5 exciting zones, each
brought to life by a unique character. This is more than an exhibition
— it’s a celebration of abilities, imagination, and joyful expression!</p>
<h4>Explore the 5 Zones:</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/createable_icon.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Purple Zone with Ms createABLE</strong>
<br>Discover powerful artworks created by talented artists with disabilities
and see the world through their eyes. Each piece tells a unique story,
filled with emotion, creativity, and personal experience.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/mr_sparkABLE.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Yellow Zone with Mr sparkABLE</strong>
<br>This is where creativity comes alive and celebrations happen in real-time!
Enjoy mesmerising live performances by artists with disabilities, or watch
as a painting unfolds before your eyes during live painting sessions with
our talented Shaping Hearts artists.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/imagineable_icon.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Blue Zone with Mr imagineABLE</strong>
<br>Join engaging workshops, explore unique handmade crafts, and discover
meaningful ways to support our artists.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/shareable_icon.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Red Zone with Ms shareABLE</strong>
<br>Get hands-on with collaborative art activities and leave your creative
mark on a special community installation! Be part of a larger shared artwork
that grows with every visitor!</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/discoverable_icon.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Green Zone with Mr discoveABLE</strong>
<br>Follow his Art Trail to uncover hidden gems tucked within murals and artworks.
Along the way, discover Singapore’s longest 132m mural — a collaborative
masterpiece co-designed by Shaping Hearts artists Simeon and Ezra, and
brought to life with the help of volunteers from SIT and fellow Shaping
Hearts artists.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The exhibition is a life-sized board game — a fun and interactive journey
for all ages! Follow the path, answer trivia questions, uncover fun facts,
and immerse yourself in the 5 vibrant zones of ARTfully Able.</p>
<p>See you there!</p>
